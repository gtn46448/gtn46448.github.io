About Me

Hello and welcome, my name is Grant! I like to channel my creativity into programming and making things that look nice. I think the best way to describe myself is half programmer, half artist... a were-programmer? Were-artist? Something like that.

I graduated from Creighton University with both a Digital Development and a Computing Science degree, so I'm still figuring out whether I want to do front-end, back-end, or full stack development. For now, let's just say that I seek to supply superb, streamlined software and establish exquisite, efficient experiences. Honestly though, the more I learn about this field, the more I see the potential for personal growth. I hope to continue to find opportunities to learn from those who seek to create great things.

When I've got a free moment, I enjoy various forms of art, literature, and Hearthstone.
