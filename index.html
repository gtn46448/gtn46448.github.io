About Me

Hello! My name is Grant and I like to channel my creativity into programming and making things that look nice. I think the best way to describe myself is half programmer, half artist... a were-programmer? Were-artist? Something like that.

I graduated from Creighton University with both a Digital Development and a Computer Science degree, so I'm still figuring out whether I want to do web development or software engineering. I hope to continue to find opportunities and learn from those who seek to create great things.

When I've got a free moment, I enjoy various forms of art, literature, and video games.

Skills

While the vast majority of my experience lies in Java programming, I am comfortable with Python and JavaScript, and I have learned the basics of PHP and SQL. I have made a game or two using Unity, and have dabbled in learning about Go and Rust.

I have a solid understanding of algorithms and data strucutres, and have had some experience working in an agile/SCRUM-like environment.

Design-wise, I have experience with HTML and CSS, and understand how to create responsive designs. I can create style guides, wireframes, and mockups in Framer and Adobe XD, and can manipulate raster and vector images in Photoshop and Illustrator. I have also done a bit of 3D modeling in Blender.
